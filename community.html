Community Engagement

Beyond formal research, I'm committed to making science accessible and engaging communities in conservation action. These projects combine hands-on stewardship with spatial analysis to connect people with the ecosystems around them.

Urban Native Gardens and Habitat Connectivity Mapping

Community Science Urban Ecology GIS Mapping

Community-led conservation combining hands-on native pollinator garden installations with spatial analysis for Friends of Coal Creek. I lead garden projects within my homeowners association while mapping certified gardens throughout the Coal Creek watershed to understand how residential native plantings create habitat connectivity.

Key Contributions: Active participant in the Pollinator City certification initiative, developing spatial tools that quantify how native gardens form stepping-stone habitat networks for urban wildlife in suburban landscapes.
